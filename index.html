ConstructionPad-DIY Support

If you need help with ConstructionPad-DIY, contact us using the information below.

Contact

Email: info@sl-harness.com

Please include your device model, iOS/iPadOS version, and a brief description of the issue.

Common Help

  • Sign in issues: Confirm you are signed in with the same Apple ID used to create your account.
  • Sync issues: Check that iCloud is enabled for the device and the App has iCloud permission.
  • Invitations / collaboration: Verify the invite email matches the email associated with your Apple Sign-In (when available).

Data & Privacy
